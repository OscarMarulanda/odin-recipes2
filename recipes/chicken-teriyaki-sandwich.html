Chicken Teriyaki Sandwich

Description

This is my favorite sandwich. I usually eat it at least once a month. I get it at subway, but now that I'm a freelancing english teacher finding my own students, I'll try and learn to make it myself.

I guess the most difficult part will be getting the right bread. Maybe I can fashion my own bread with a regular baguette, some garlic and oregano, and a little butter.

These are the ingredients you'll need to make me a sandwich:

  • a baguette
  • some slices of cheese
  • some sliced bell peppers
  • a sliced tomato
  • one single slice of a jalape√±o
  • chopped lettuce
  • sliced pickles
  • half a chicken breast, diced
  • teriyaki sauce
  • chipotle sauce
  • sweet onion sauce
  • olive oil
  • an onion, sliced

These is how you make this delicious sandwich:

  1. slice the bread open
  2. put some chicken and cheese inside the bread
  3. put bread, chicken, and cheese inside the oven for 1 minute, just enough to get it toasty
  4. take sandwich out of the oven and add vegetables. Be careful not to add too much of any of the vegetables as this won't leave enough room for the others.
  5. add sauces to taste

Your sandwich is ready! you can pair it with a cold glass of mango or passion fruit juice and a cookie :)
