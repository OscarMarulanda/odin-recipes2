White Russian

Description

This is a cocktail that I first heard of in a movie, The Great Lebowski. It is a movie about a guy who gets mixed up in dangerous business because he has the same name as another man.

This cocktail caught my attention, and I suppose everybody who saw the movie's attention, because the main character keeps ordering it over and over, everywhere he goes.

He is a pretty chill dude. This is actually what he likes to be called, the dude. Anyway...

For this cocktail you'll need to following:

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 1 ounce Kahlúa
  • 1 splash heavy cream
  • Love

Steps

  1. Add the vodka and Kahlúa to a rocks glass filled with ice.
  2. Top with the heavy cream and enjoy.

Drink with love and have a great time.
