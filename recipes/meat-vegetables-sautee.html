Meat and vegetables sauté

Description

This is one of my favorite dishes. I usually eat it with my girlfriend. We make it together; It is a great recipe because it is relatively simple to make and I think it is healthy - it has lots of protein and vegetables, and you can pair it with rice to get some carbs, too.

When I make this dish with my girlfriend, I chop up the vegetables and she is in charge of sauteing everything and adding the sauces. I hope you have someone to make this dish with, too :3

These are the ingredients you'll need to make me a sandwich:

  • half a pound of beef, chopped
  • some sliced bell peppers
  • one tomato, diced, unpeeled
  • chopped lettuce
  • teriyaki sauce
  • soy sauce
  • olive oil
  • an onion, sliced
  • a sliced cucumber
  • a little cilantro

These is how you make this delicious dish:

  1. First you need to fry the meat a little so it is tender when you add the rest
  2. When the meat is soft and a little cooked, add the vegetables
  3. mix everything together and stir until it's all juicy

Your sauté is ready! you can pair it with rice or mashed potatoes and a cold glass of mango or passion fruit juice and a cookie :)
